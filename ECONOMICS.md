# Unit Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal: a 10-person startup spending $500/mo on AI tools
- Credex discount: 20-30% off retail → customer saves ~$100-150/mo
- Credex margin: ~15% of the credits sold
- Average contract value: $500/mo × 12 months = $6,000/year in credits sold
- Credex revenue per customer: $6,000 × 15% = **$900/year per customer**
- If customer stays 2 years: **LTV = ~$1,800**

## CAC at each GTM channel

| Channel | Effort | Est. Conversions | Est. CAC |
|---------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| HN Show HN post | 1 hour | 3-5 Credex leads | ~$0 |
| Reddit posts | 30 min each | 1-2 leads per post | ~$0 |
| Cursor Discord | 20 min | 1-2 leads | ~$0 |
| LinkedIn cold DM (20 CTOs) | 2 hours | 1-2 leads | ~$0 |
| Referral from audit URL shares | Passive | 1-3 leads/week | ~$0 |

All channels are $0 paid budget. Real cost is founder time (~$50/hour opportunity cost).
Effective CAC at launch: **$100-200 in time cost per converted Credex customer.**

## Conversion funnel

| Stage | Rate | Numbers |
|-------|------|---------|
| Visitor lands on page | 100% | 1,000 visitors/month |
| Completes audit | 25% | 250 audits |
| Sees >$500/mo savings | 30% | 75 users |
| Clicks Credex CTA | 20% | 15 clicks |
| Books consultation | 40% | 6 consultations |
| Becomes paying customer | 50% | 3 customers/month |

At $900/year LTV: **3 customers/month = $2,700/month = $32,400 ARR** from 1,000 visitors/month.

## What makes this profitable
CAC of ~$150 vs LTV of $1,800 = **LTV:CAC ratio of 12x** — well above the 3x threshold 
for a healthy SaaS business. The tool pays for itself after the first customer.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

Month 1-3: 500 visitors/month → 1-2 customers/month → $1,800-3,600 ARR
Month 4-6: HN traction + word of mouth → 2,000 visitors/month → 5-6 customers/month
Month 7-12: SEO kicks in, audit URLs shared → 5,000 visitors/month → 12-15 customers/month → ~$150,000 ARR
Month 13-18: Credex sales team uses tool as outbound hook → 10,000 visitors/month → 
30 customers/month → $32,400/month → **$388,000 ARR**

To hit $1M ARR in 18 months requires either:
- 80 active customers at $12,500 ACV (larger enterprise deals), OR
- 1,100 customers at $900 ACV (requires significant scale), OR
- **Most realistic: hybrid** — 30 SMB customers ($900 ACV) + 10 mid-market customers 
  ($5,000 ACV) + Credex upsells on credits = ~$1M ARR

Key assumptions that must hold:
1. Audit-to-consultation conversion stays above 5%
2. Credex closes at least 40% of consultations
3. Average deal size grows as Credex adds more credit types
4. Tool drives organic sharing (audit URLs go viral in at least 1 community)
